# 43. Closing a restaurant down

**What this chapter settles:** how to close one restaurant permanently without moving its life into the rest of the chain or changing the chain's promise.

## This close ends one restaurant for good

A permanent closure is not the close at the end of service. **It ends this restaurant rather
than preparing it for another day.** Chapter 23 handles the nightly close. Do not repeat that
work here or mistake a restaurant that will open tomorrow for one that has ended.

The chain may continue after one restaurant closes. The remaining restaurants still make the
same promise from the same manual.

## Close the restaurant without moving its life

Close it in this order.

1. End this restaurant's service for good.
2. Add the closure to the end of its operations log as the final event.
3. Leave its operations log and stocktake with the restaurant.
4. Leave its building, stock and staff with the restaurant.
5. End its local menu, then stop including this restaurant in the area manager's weekly read.

**Nothing owned or lived moves to another restaurant.** Moving it would give another place a
building, stock, staff or past that was never its own.

## The two records stay where their facts happened

The operations log is the diary of what happened in this restaurant. The stocktake is the
photograph of what this restaurant has. **Both stay with the restaurant they describe.** They
do not move to another restaurant, enter the chain's records or become one combined record.

Closure does not give you permission to improve the diary. Add that the restaurant closed to
the end of the log. Do not edit, shorten or rearrange what came before. The closure is the
last thing that happened there, so it becomes the last entry.

The stocktake remains a separate photograph. It stays local and is not transferred to a
remaining restaurant.

## The manual outlives one restaurant

The recipes, house rules, taster's checklist and job descriptions remain in the static chain
manual. **Closing one restaurant changes none of these travelling instructions.** They still
carry the promise made by every restaurant that remains open.

The local menu is different. It belonged only to the closing restaurant, so it ceases with
that restaurant. It never travelled while the restaurant was open and does not travel now.
Do not preserve it in the chain manual. The chain's recipes remain there unchanged.

## The weekly read leaves the closed restaurant behind

The area manager no longer includes the closed restaurant's log when reading across the
chain each week. That does not move or erase the log. **Each log remains with its own
restaurant, even after that restaurant closes.** Chapter 38 explains the weekly read; the
closure changes only which restaurants still take part.

## Never tidy the diary at the end

A final clean copy may look like a respectful way to finish. It is not. **Rewriting the log
would replace what the restaurant lived with a later story about it.** Add the closure once,
at the end, and leave every earlier entry untouched.

---

## The rules, flat

*The same rules as above, with the explanation removed. This is the part the appliance
obeys.*

1. Treat permanent closure as the end of one restaurant, never as its nightly close.
2. End the restaurant's service for good.
3. Add the closure as the final entry in that restaurant's operations log. Never edit,
   shorten or rearrange an earlier entry.
4. Keep the closed restaurant's operations log and stocktake with that restaurant. Never
   transfer or merge either record into another restaurant's records or the chain's records.
5. Keep the closed restaurant's building, stock and staff local. Never move them to another
   restaurant.
6. Leave the recipes, house rules, taster's checklist and job descriptions unchanged in the
   static chain manual.
7. Let the closing restaurant's local menu cease with it. Never move that menu into another
   restaurant or preserve it in the chain manual.
8. Stop including the closed restaurant's log in the area manager's weekly read. Leave the
   log with the closed restaurant.
9. Continue the remaining chain under the same promise and the same manual.
